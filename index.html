What is FISSEA?
Introduction:

FISSEA stands for Federal Information Security Educators. It is an organization run by and for the Federal Government. FISSEA was founded in 1987.

Goals: :

Their purpose is to advance the general level of knowledge in information security for the federal government and its affiliates.
